Join Us at RE: Random Blends 2018!

Since its inception in 2009, Random Blends has provided a platform for NUS students to express their talents and creativity by showcasing projects that bring art, design and new media together in innovative ways.

This year, Random Blends celebrates its 10th anniversary with RE:. A mash-up of the prefix ‘re’, commonly used in the environmental slogan “reduce, reuse and recycle” and the email preposition ‘Re:’ that denotes a reply or a conversation, RE: aims to explore our relationships and responses to the environments we inhabit, where we hope to evoke thoughts, feelings and hopes about environmental sustainability, technological innovation and social issues.

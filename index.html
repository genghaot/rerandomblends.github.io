Join Us at RE: Random Blends 2018!

Since its inception in 2009, Random Blends has provided a platform for NUS students to express their talents and creativity by showcasing projects that bring art, design and new media together in innovative ways.

This year, Random Blends celebrates its 10th anniversary with RE:. A mash-up of the prefix ‘re’, commonly used in the environmental slogan “reduce, reuse and recycle” and the email preposition ‘Re:’ that denotes a reply, RE: explores our relationships and responses to the environments we inhabit. We hope to evoke thoughts, feelings and hopes about:

Environmental Sustainability

Social Issues

Technological Innovation

Workshops & Activities

Redefining a Book Swap

Come together with book lovers to exchange your books for other pre-loved reads. All you need is a book or two and an open mind!

Make-Your-Own Terrarium Workshop

Reconnect with nature by building your own terrarium with recycled materials and your creativity. No green thumbs needed to attend this workshop led by CNM students!

Sale of BYO Merchandise

The Sustainability Project will be selling a range of high-quality and affordable products to bring you one step closer to a Bring-Your-Own (BYO) lifestyle!
